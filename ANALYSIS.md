# 3. Geographic Distribution

2023

Minimal or no contribution from international markets

2024

Continued dominance of Turkey

Global (online)

France

# 4. Brand Performance Mix

2023

2024

Revenue distribution becomes more balanced

Insight:
Brand portfolio diversification improved in 2024, reducing dependency on a single brand and lowering commercial risk.
